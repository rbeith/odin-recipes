Sandwich Recipe

Recipe Details

Sandwiches are great but the easiest of all is sometimes the best.

Whenever your in a pinch, you can't go wrong with that childhood classic: PBandJ.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • peanut butter
  • a fine jam

Steps

  1. spread peanut butter evenly on both slices of bread
  2. add a modest amount of jam. too much and it will smoosh all over.
  3. gently press the two slices of bread together.
