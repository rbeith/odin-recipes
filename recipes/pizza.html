Pizza Recipe

Recipe Details

Here's my pizza recipe. It's taken some time to perfect, but I think I've finally nailed it.

A few notes to make sure everything is perfect. First, age the dough in the fridge for at least two days. Second, use good canned tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • all purpose flour
  • high protein bread flour
  • salt, yeast, water, oil

Steps

  1. make the dough and let it rest for 2-3 days in the fridge
  2. let the dough rise at room temp for a couple hours
  3. pizzafy the dough into a disc
  4. add toppings and bake as hot as you can
